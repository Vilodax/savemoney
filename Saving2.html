❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum saving amount?

The minimum saving amount is 5,000 UGX. You can save up to 1,000,000 UGX per transaction.

How long does it take to confirm my savings?

Your savings are confirmed within 24 hours after submission and verification by our treasurer.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN/Airtel), Bank Transfers, Cash payments, and Online Transfers.

Can I withdraw my savings anytime?

Yes, you can withdraw your savings after completing 3 months of membership. Some conditions may apply.

Do you offer loans to members?

Yes! Members can access emergency loans at competitive rates. Contact the treasurer for more details.

How is interest calculated?

Interest is calculated quarterly on your total savings balance. Current rate: 5% per annum.

How can I join the group?

Contact any group official. You need to register, pay a one-time registration fee of 10,000 UGX, and make your first savings deposit.
